Lingo

A data visualization that dynamically represents the most common slang word used on twitter in a live snapshot. Lingo measures the use of slang words in 5 different English-speaking cities around the world and allows the user to compare slang use in each. Users can also input their own slang to be approved by the content manager.
In collaboration with Jennifer Vandermeer, Adalyn Ordono, and Natalia Bagan.

Kickster

A tiny game as an introduction to multiuser applications and Node.js. The first participant becomes the "kicker", and once the chat is full, has the opportunity to kick one of their friends out of the conversation every thirty seconds. This friend is locked out until everyone else leaves the conversation. Currently not online, but can be played locally from the source files if Node.js, Express, and Socket.io are installed on the local machine.
In collaboration with Jennifer Vandermeer.

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • jQuery
  • Node.js
  • Socket.io
